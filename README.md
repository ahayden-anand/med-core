#  MedCore – Hospital Appointment Booking System

![MedCore Project Preview](./project3.png)

MedCore is a comprehensive hospital appointment booking solution designed to simplify and streamline patient–doctor scheduling while improving operational efficiency for healthcare providers.

---

##  Overview

**MedCore** enables patients to easily **book, reschedule, or cancel appointments** through a simple and intuitive interface. At the same time, it provides hospitals and clinics with structured tools to manage **doctors, schedules, availability, and patient records** efficiently.

The system focuses on reducing waiting times, minimizing scheduling conflicts, and improving overall patient experience through digital automation.

---

##  Key Features

###  Patient Features
-  Book, reschedule, or cancel appointments easily  
-  View available doctors and time slots  
-  Access appointment details and history  
-  Reduced waiting time and manual coordination  

###  Hospital & Clinic Features
-  Doctor and department management  
-  Availability and schedule control  
-  Efficient appointment tracking  
-  Patient record management  

---

##  Project Objectives

- Simplify hospital appointment scheduling  
- Improve patient accessibility to healthcare services  
- Reduce administrative workload for clinics
